## What This Blog Covers

Europe faces two inseparable challenges:

### Digital Infrastructure Sovereignty

From processors to operating systems, from data centers to energy systems - Europe must control its own technological stack. Current dependence on US cloud providers and Chinese hardware creates strategic vulnerability.

### Defense & Security Sovereignty

European military autonomy is impossible while running on American infrastructure. You cannot defend your territory when your command systems, intelligence processing, and secure communications depend on others' clouds and processors.

### Economic Transformation

Both pillars require - and enable - massive economic opportunity: European tech jobs, strategic industries, reduced licensing costs, innovation ecosystems.

## A Recent Example

My home country, the Netherlands, created **ASML** - the only company in the world that makes machines for advanced computer chips. Dutch technology. Dutch engineering. Global strategic importance.

But when the United States decided ASML couldn't sell to China, the **Dutch government complied** - losing billions in sales.

American control of Dutch technology.

That pattern - European technological excellence without European strategic autonomy - is what this blog addresses.
